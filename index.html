Astronomy & Data Analysis

I am Rohit, an astrophysicist at the Pennsylvania State University. My work involves characterization of stars that may host habitable planets. Habitable planets are rocky planets that have just the right conditions to support life. I am part of the team looking for Earth 2.0. My work involves working on datasets from large Astronomical surveys such as SDSS. I am currently involved in the SDSS-III project that looks at 100,000 stars. You can read more about my work, my skill sets and expertise under the About page.

Data Analysis is part of research work. Primarily, I use Python and do most of data munging in Pandas. Check out my Project page for recent work and Blog posts for things to learn.
