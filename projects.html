Projects

I have a lot of ideas and therefore I decide to dedicate this page to include these ideas. Furthermore, these ideas become projects, side-projects, and even tutorials. I am particularly interested in Data Analysis tool called Python and visualization tool called D3.

I must agree that D3, which stands for Data-Driven Documents (should be called D-cube), has mind-blowing animation. However, learning is often quite excruciating for newbies. I've been learning it for the past two months. Hence, I try to incorporate much of my visulization in D3.

If you got to this page by chance, do explore various projects I've been working on. I hope you will learn something new or even teach me. If you like to know exciting ideas I will sculpt in the future, do follow me on Twitter.

Visualizing Hipparcos Stars with D3

April 1, 2014

While learning D3, I really wanted to work on a large dataset and follow the 4-step process of Data Analysis. Therefore, I chose to work on 186,000 stars from the Hipparcos catalogue. This project, completely written in iPython Notebook, illustrates how I collect, clean, and plot the data in Python with Matplotlib and D3. The D3 visualization is done on a separate page.

Mining ADS NASA API for First Planet Discovery Papers

March 03, 2014

I am affiliated with the Center of Exoplanets and Habitable Worlds at Penn State. As a member, I wanted to find out the expertise the members possess and the substantial contribution they have made over the years. In this project, I illustrate their contribution by mining the ADS NASA database and find the number of first planet discovery papers they are involved in.
